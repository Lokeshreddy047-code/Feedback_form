About Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) is a private engineering college located in Gandipet, near Financial District, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The college is affiliated to Osmania University[1] and is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation.[2] The institute received an autonomous status in 2013.
